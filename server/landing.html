Features

Everything you need to run a professional newsletter

From writing to analytics, Letterflow gives you the complete toolkit to grow and monetize your audience.

Newsletter Editor

Rich text block editor with drag-and-drop sections, inline image uploads, reusable templates, and mobile preview. Supports HTML export and scheduled publishing.

Subscriber Management

Import subscribers via CSV or API. Create segments by tags, signup source, engagement level, or custom fields. Manage unsubscribes and bounces automatically.

Email Sending

Send campaigns via Resend or AWS SES. SPF/DKIM setup wizard, deliverability scoring, send-time optimization, and bounce/complaint handling with automatic list hygiene.

Analytics Dashboard

Per-campaign metrics: open rate, click rate, unsubscribes, and growth trends. Subscriber growth chart, geo distribution, and top-performing content ranking over 7/30/90-day windows.

Automation Sequences

Build multi-step drip campaigns triggered by signup, tag assignment, or date. Visual sequence builder with delay nodes, conditional branches, and per-step performance metrics.

Landing Pages

Drag-and-drop landing page builder for newsletter signups. Custom domains, embedded forms, social proof widgets, and conversion tracking. Mobile-responsive out of the box.

A/B Testing

Split-test subject lines, sender names, content blocks, and send times. Automatic winner selection based on open rate or click rate after a configurable sample window.

Import/Export

One-click import from Mailchimp, Substack, ConvertKit, or CSV. Export full subscriber lists and campaign history as CSV/JSON. GDPR-compliant data portability.
